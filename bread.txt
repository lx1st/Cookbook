CHAPTER 1. BREAD
Recipe 1.1: Basic bread
Ingreditents:
- flour (4 cups)
- salt (1 teaspoon)
- water (600 ml)
- yeast (1 teaspoon)
Instructions:
- mix ingredients
- knead until the dough becoms stretchy (about 15 minutes)
- let rise for 1 hour
- let rise for 25 minutes
- bake at 425 F for 25 minutes
